The Rules of Shirt Supremacy

On the Fourth of July, there exists one sacred and universally acknowledged truth: someone must have the best shirt.

The Best Shirt is determined by a combination of patriotic spirit, visual impact, unapologetic confidence, and the ability to make other shirts feel inadequate simply by standing nearby.

Bald eagles, fireworks, flags, historical figures, explosions, irony, and excessive freedom are all encouraged. Subtlety is not.

Final judgment is absolute, non-negotiable, and delivered in the spirit of liberty. Complaints will be ignored in the name of democracy.
